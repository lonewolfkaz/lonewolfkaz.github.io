Yoomoney ex. Yandex.Checkout

2019 – 2021

Yandex.Checkout — A fintech service for accepting online and offline payments. Stripe-ish. With MAU 10k merchants. Yandex.Checkout has been the most popular payment application in Russia since 2015.

In December 2020 Yandex.Checkout was renamed to YooMoney.

I joined Yandex in 2019 as a product designer and grew to a senior product designer. Over two years period, I worked on various web and mobile projects in Yandex.Checkout and Yandex.Money.
